What we do

Six pillars that take you from curious first-year to confident graduate.

Markets & Trading

Weekly markets briefings and a student-run trading challenge

Industry Networking

Mixers with banks, funds and Big Four firms each semester

Technical Workshops

Excel, financial modelling, valuation and Python for finance

1:1 Mentorship

Matched with an industry mentor from your target career path

Case Competitions

Compete in our annual M&A and equity research challenges

Socials & Community

End-of-exam parties, trivia nights and an annual gala dinner
